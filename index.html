The present study

Research Question

  1. Do L1 English L2 Spanish learners in an immersion program demonstrate less centralization of unstressed /a/ over the course of six weeks?

participants

10 L1 English L2 Spanish upper A2 learners 6 week immersion program

materials & task

participants shadowed individual words

126 words that they believed to be Spanish

They repeated each word 3 times, for a total of 378 words per participant

3780 words in total

All words were bisyllabic paroxytones

The final vowel was always unstressed /a/

Some example stimuli

gaka

gota

ita

kaka

Formant extraction

Montreal Forced Aligner. Corrections have started.

FastTrack (Barreda, 2021).

Set to 3 formants (so it prioritizes the first 3 formants).

Extracts values every 2 ms.

De la Fuente Iglesias & Pérez Castillejo (2022); Amengual & Simonet (2020)

data processing

Vowels were of different durations, so…

  1. time was normalized
  2. normalized times were binned into 11 bins
  3. f1 and f2 averaged for each bin
  4. plotted to f1xf2 space to see trajectory
